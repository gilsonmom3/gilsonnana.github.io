Your Path Starts Here

You don't need to understand everything on Day 1. You just need to start. Here's the simple path I walk every new teammate through:

Enroll as a Wellness Partner

Choose your Launch Pack level. This activates your partner account and unlocks your earning potential immediately — including the Heart Start Bonus from day one.

Get Active (100 PV/month on Subscribe & Save)

Maintain a 100 PV monthly Subscribe & Save order to stay commission-qualified each month. This is what keeps all your bonuses "on."

Enroll 3 Customers or Partners

Share the products with people you know. Enroll 3 people with 100+ PV orders and unlock the Me & Three Bonus — covering your own product cost.

Teach Your 3 to Enroll 3

When your three people each do the same thing — boom, the Me & Three Team Bonus adds an extra $250/month on top. And your Unilevel residuals begin layering in.

Advance in Rank — Unlock Bigger Pools

As your team grows, your Organization Volume grows, and you advance in rank. Each rank unlocks deeper Unilevel levels, bigger Pool shares, and eventually the Minimum Income Guarantee at Silver.

Build to Leader & Diamond — Legacy Income

At Diamond, commissions flow to unlimited depth in your organization. This is where legacy, generational income is built. Every person below you contributes to your check — forever.
